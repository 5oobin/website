Hello!

I'm a second-year PhD student in Human Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Prof. David McDonald and Prof. Mark Zachry.

As a qualitative HCI and UX researcher, I explore user behavior and design new systems, recently focusing on users building social dynamics with and through AI.

I previously earned my M.S. in Intelligence and Information from Seoul National University and my B.A. from Ewha Womans University. I also worked as a UX researcher at Soft Berry Inc.

Focus Areas: Human-AI Interaction, Human-Agent Interaction, Social Computing, Computer Supported Cooperative Work
